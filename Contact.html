History on Your Doorstep is run by Brigid Fice

Brigid has an Advanced Diploma in Local History from Oxford University and has been researching house histories for 15 years.

She is also Vice Chairman of the charitable organisation the Domestic Buildings Research Group (Surrey) which records old buildings in and around Surrey.

Her special interest is in combining the architecture of the buildings with the lives of the people who owned and lived in them. Bringing their stories to life in a fully illustrated and referenced book can help owners understand changes they would like to make, enhance the value of the property or simply help to enjoy their homes.
