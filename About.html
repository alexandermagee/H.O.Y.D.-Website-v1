What about the construction and architecural features?

The secrets of a house's history, especially an older one, can be revealed by the building itself.

By assessing the architectural features and combining this with documentary evidence, it is often possible to establish the date of construction to within 10 years.

With the additional option of dating timbers with dendrochronology, you could find out the actual year it was built.
