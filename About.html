We build up a picture by assessing the architectural features and interpreting the documentary evidence.

Each area has its own datable architetural features and each property has its own surviving documents. Piecing all these together enables us to tell a house's story.

With the additional option of dating timbers with dendrochronology, you could find out the actual year it was built.
